Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

E.K. InteriorDesign ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data when you visit our website or contact us about our interior design services.

1. Who We Are

E.K. InteriorDesign is an interior design studio based in Prishtina, Kosovo. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, you can reach us at info@ekinteriordesign.com or +383 48 367 833.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide directly: such as your name, email address, phone number, and any details you share when you contact us, request a quote, or discuss a project with us.
  • Automatically collected information: such as your IP address, browser type, device information, and general browsing behavior on our site, collected through cookies and similar technologies (see our Cookie Policy for details).
  • Project-related information: such as photos, floor plans, measurements, or preferences you share with us to help design your space.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide quotes or consultations
  • Plan, design, and deliver interior design projects you've requested
  • Communicate with you about your project's progress
  • Improve our website and services
  • Showcase completed projects in our portfolio, only with your consent
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted third parties who help us operate our business, such as suppliers, contractors, or service providers involved in delivering your project, and only to the extent necessary. We may also disclose information if required by law.

5. Social Media & Third-Party Links

Our website links to our Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok pages. These platforms have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them, as we are not responsible for how they handle your data.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, such as completing a project or responding to your inquiry, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

8. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your information
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@ekinteriordesign.com.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
