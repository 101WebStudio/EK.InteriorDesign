Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how E.K. InteriorDesign ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember information about your visit, such as your preferences, which can make your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • Essential functionality: to make our website work properly, such as remembering your display preference (light or dark mode) using your browser's local storage.
  • Performance and analytics: to understand how visitors use our site, so we can improve content and navigation.
  • Embedded content: resources loaded from third parties, such as icon libraries, may set their own cookies or use similar technologies.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Type Purpose
Strictly Necessary Required for the website to function properly (e.g. navigation, security).
Preference Remembers choices you make, such as light/dark mode, to improve your experience.
Analytics Helps us understand how visitors interact with our website so we can improve it.
Third-Party Set by external services we use, such as content delivery networks or social media links.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some content on our website, such as icon libraries and social media links (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok), is provided by third parties who may set their own cookies. We do not control these cookies, and we encourage you to review the relevant third party's cookie policy for more information.

5. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can usually choose to block or delete cookies, though doing so may affect the functionality of our website. You can find out more about managing cookies at aboutcookies.org.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our practices. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
